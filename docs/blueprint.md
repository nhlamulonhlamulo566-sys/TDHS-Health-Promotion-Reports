# **App Name**: HealthReports

## Core Features:

- Dashboard: Display an overview of key health reporting activities, including weekly plans, people reached, health talks, and reports filed.
- Weekly Plans: Allow users to create and manage weekly health plans.
- Health Talks: Enable users to log and track health talks conducted.
- Health Campaigns: Manage and monitor health campaigns, potentially using AI to suggest optimal outreach strategies based on community data.
- IMC Tracking: Allow users to track and analyze the IMC (integrated management of childhood illness) data.
- Schools Integration: Integrate with schools to collect and report on student health data. The AI component will serve as a tool that helps identify key risks and offer plans.
- Reports Generation: Generate summary health reports with visualizations for different metrics, using an AI to provide insight and guide decision-making in healthcare.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#A0D2EB) to convey calmness and health.
- Background color: Light grey (#F5F5F5) for a clean, professional look.
- Accent color: Light orange (#FFB347) to highlight important actions and information.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif suitable for body text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a geometric sans-serif font with a computerized feel; for headers and titles.
- Use simple, consistent icons to represent different health metrics and actions.
- Clean and organized layout with a left sidebar for navigation and a main content area for displaying data.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback on user interactions.
